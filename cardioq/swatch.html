The heart gradient — cobalt → azure → cyan. Use for hero surfaces, the logo lockup, charts and the primary accent. Bring in the pulse red only for vitals / alerts / the ECG motif — never as a fill.

Dark & light themes

rules

Usage

Blue leadsAzure #1F6FE5 is the primary; cyan #22C3ED is the signal / secondary. The brand is dark-native — design dark-first, then light.
Pulse is a signalCoral-red #FB5249 = the heartbeat: vitals, alerts, the ECG line, live states. High-impact, low-frequency.
Don't flood with redNever use pulse as a background or large fill — at scale it reads as error / danger.
Don't blend with ZiffyHealth greenCardioQ = blue / cyan / coral; ZiffyHealth = green. Keep the systems separate; co-brand with logos, not by mixing palettes.
